Richard Vale

I am interested in data and mathematical modelling. I have a PhD in mathematics from the University of Glasgow (2007) and was an HC Wang Assistant Professor at Cornell University (2007-10). Previously I studied at the University of Auckland and the University of Cambridge (Part III Mathematics, aka M.A.St 2003). I have also taught at Oberlin College (2010-11), the University of Auckland, AUT and the University of Canterbury (2014).
