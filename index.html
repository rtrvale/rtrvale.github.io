Richard Vale

I am currently (2016) working as a Data Scientist. In 2014 I was a Lecturer in the Statistics Department at the University of Canterbury. I have a PhD in mathematics from the University of Glasgow (2007) and was an HC Wang Assistant Professor at Cornell University (2007-10). Previously I studied at the University of Auckland and the University of Cambridge (Part III Mathematics, aka M.A.St 2003). I have also taught at Oberlin College (2010-11), the University of Auckland, and AUT.

Other

Recently (2015) I made an experimental word cloud from the text of The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes. Words which occur close together in the text should occur close together in the cloud. Conventional word clouds are just a way of showing how many times each word appears, so this is an interesting alternative.
